Elara had never meant to disturb the old chest beneath her grandmother’s floorboards, yet there it lay open before her, breathing dust into the narrow room. Inside, beneath yellowed letters and a broken silver clasp, she found a folded map inked with twisting paths and symbols she did not know. At its center, written in faded green script, were the words The Whispering Woods Chronicle. As the evening wind rattled the shutters, Elara felt a strange pull deep in her chest, as though the map had been waiting not merely to be found, but to be claimed by her alone.
