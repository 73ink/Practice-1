Elara is a young villager with chestnut-brown hair, thoughtful green eyes, and a determined expression that makes her seem older than her years. Though raised in a quiet village, she is brave, curious, and unable to ignore the strange mystery of the map she has discovered.

Shadow is a loyal wolf with thick black fur, bright silver eyes, and a calm, watchful nature. Fiercely protective of Elara, he moves silently through the woods and seems to understand dangers before they appear.
